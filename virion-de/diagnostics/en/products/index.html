We are the IVD product Experts of Virion\Serion

SERION Diagnostics

40 years of experience and specializing in the development and manufacture of in-vitro diagnostics with more than 120 employees ensure supply of high quality solutions for IVD products.

Our product portfolio comprises different test systems for the serological detection of infectious diseases, which can be caused by various viruses, bacteria, parasites or fungi. With more than 100 different parameters we offer one of the largest ELISA portfolios for the diagnosis of infectious diseases worldwide including rare parameters such as Francisella tularensis, Coxiella burnetii and Brucella. For a comfortable, cost and time-efficient processing, we offer modern automation solutions as well as detailed and user-friendly software tools.

Our Product Ranges

SERION Diagnostics offers test systems for diagnosis of infectious diseases (SERION ELISA classic / antigen, SERION CFT), SERION controls for your internal quality control, automation solutions and software tools for easy handling. Besides the standard serology application various SERION ELISA classic tests allow the detection of intrathecal synthesized antibodies in cerebrospinal fluid for CSF diagnostics, the determination of IgG antibody avidity for the differentiation of acute from recent infections and are suitable for newborn screening with dried blood spots (DBS).
