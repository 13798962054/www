SERION antibodies

Cytomegalovirus (CMV)

Humanized monoclonal anti-Cytomegalovirus (CMV) IgM antibody, positive for Cytomegalovirus (CMV), manufactured in hybridoma cell culture. Perfectly suited as an alternative to human sera/plasma for the manufacturing of calibrators and controls.

Bulk volumes are available and 0.5 mL test samples can be offered free of charge at any time.
